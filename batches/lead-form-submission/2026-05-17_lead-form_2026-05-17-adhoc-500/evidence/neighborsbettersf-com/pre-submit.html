Neighbors for a Better San Francisco is an organization of dedicated San Franciscans committed to public safety, serious solutions to homelessness, high-quality public education, fiscal responsibility, and good government for our city.

Our Accomplishments

San Francisco’s renaissance is possible only with talented, capable, bold leaders in local government. We are dedicated to cultivating, supporting, and electing those leaders to move our city forward.

We are proud to have made significant progress in the last four years:

Led the successful recall of DA Chesa Boudin, a catalyst for the city in restoring public safety.

Funded and helped organize the successful recall of three school board members who failed our students, bankrupted our school district, and kept our schools closed.

Supported a centrist majority on our Board of Supervisors to restore public safety, address homelessness, and revitalize downtown.

Supported public safety champion District Attorney Brooke Jenkins.

Supported a centrist majority on the Board of Education to push forward necessary fiscal and student-centered reforms.

Restored public safety and public education through ballot measures campaigns such as Stop the Cop Tax (2024), Yes on Prop 36 (2024), and Restore 8th Grade Algebra (2024).
