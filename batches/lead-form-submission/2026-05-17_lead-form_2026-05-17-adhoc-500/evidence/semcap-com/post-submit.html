    WE INVEST IN SEMINAL TRENDS SHAPING THE FUTURE

    SEMCAP is a growth equity platform, investing in companies at the forefront of seminal trends in sectors that have the greatest impact on society -
    AI, Beauty & Wellness, Food & Nutrition and Healthcare.

    WHAT WE DO

    OUR EXPERIENCE

    Our goal is to create enduring value and generate above-market returns for our global investor base by partnering with entrepreneurs driving seminal trends that are underpinned by technological, social and environmental transformation and regulatory change.

    Through SEMCAP’s capital, networks and expertise, we empower innovators to scale rapidly and deliver measurable positive outcomes for future generations.  We have operator DNA having run businesses in each of our verticals and provide unique insights, perspectives and connections to the entrepreneurs we partner with.

    SEMCAP is grounded in three decades of successful technology investments made in response to seminal trends. SEMCAP’s founders Buck Buckley and Cyrus Vandrevala are both serial entrepreneurs and eminent technology investors who have built, scaled, sold and backed many transformative companies over the past 30 years. SEMCAP was created to complement our technology expertise and expand the reach - we've curated industry leading teams and globally recognized leaders and advisory boards across each of our four investment verticals to identify and support remarkable companies that are driving seminal trends.

    "We strive to invest in great businesses where our strategic input, networks and operational experience can drive transformation," Buck Buckley, Co-Founder and CIO

    "Seminal trends will drive a paradigm shift for future generations - we back innovators driving change,"
    Cyrus Vandrevala, Co-Founder and CIO

    INVESTMENT THEMES

    We identify, evaluate and invest in industry sectors whose transformation will have the greatest impact over the next century. Seminal trends impacted by government policy, technology and shifting consumer values in the following four areas are reframing the way business is conducted and the way in which people live:

    Global transformation:

    • AI powered SaaS solutions driving exponential productivity and/or leveraging data as a moat

    • Solutions that apply ML to vertical businesses

    • Solutions that provide data for business workflow, decision making and/or enable better management of data risk and governance

    • Solutions integrating AI software with hardware

    Beauty and wellness platforms positioned at the intersection of science, technology and longevity:

    • Skincare, cosmetics, fragrance, haircare and personalized health

    • Solutions where innovation is supported by data, diagnostics and technological advancement

    Shifting values and consumer preferences:

    • Nutrient dense, functional ingredients, clean label

    • Organic, non-GMO, regenerative

    • Sustainable, plant-based or low waste

    • Convenient omni channel and DTC access

    Transformative healthcare and outcomes:

    • Payor, Provider and Pharma Tech & Services

    • Care Model Innovation

    • The New Healthcare Consumer

    • Home Based, Digital and Virtual Care
