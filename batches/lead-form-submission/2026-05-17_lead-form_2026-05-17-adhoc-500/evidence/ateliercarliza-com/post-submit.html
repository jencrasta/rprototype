Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take?

You'll receive a proof within 24-48 hours. Once approved, standard production takes 2-3 weeks. Rush options are available for faster delivery.

What do I need to provide?

Simply send us a photo or rendering of your subject (property, pet, design concept, etc.). We'll handle the rest and provide a mockup for your approval.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes! While we are based in Flower Mound, TX, we ship custom pieces to clients across the United States. Shipping costs vary by size and location.

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely! We work with you during the proof stage to ensure the piece meets your expectations. We'll make adjustments until you are completely satisfied.
