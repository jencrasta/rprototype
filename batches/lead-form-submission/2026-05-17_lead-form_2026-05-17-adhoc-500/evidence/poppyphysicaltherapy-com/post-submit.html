About Poppy Physical Therapy

Pelvic floor issues, a common affliction of both men and women, can have a profound effect on how you live your life. Poppy Physical Therapy was founded to provide top quality care for everyone with pelvic floor dysfunction, to help them get back to doing what they love.

At Poppy Physical Therapy, our objective is to identify the root cause of your problem, and provide you with the tools you need for effective, long-lasting relief.
