    Welcome to Dress for Success Greater London

    Workforce | Women | Wardrobe

    Dress for Success Greater London exists to empower women from positions of disadvantage into the workplace. We provide workplace clothing and styling, interview coaching and ongoing support to help women achieve economic independence and build the life they deserve.

    Find Out More

    Who Do We Support?

    Founded by our Chair, Juanita Ingram, Esq., we are a network partner of the US-based Dress for Success Worldwide, which has been at the forefront of women’s economic development for almost three decades. Like our American counterpart, we promote the empowerment, economic independence and social mobility of marginalised women, including prison leavers, care leavers, survivors of domestic abuse, NEET youth and single parents.

    What We Do

    Professional Attire

    We give women in need interview clothing and appropriate workwear to help them to enter or re-enter the workplace.
