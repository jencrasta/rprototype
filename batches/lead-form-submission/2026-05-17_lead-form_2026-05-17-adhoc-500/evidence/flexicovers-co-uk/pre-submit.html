WORLD CLASS OEM

The UK’s number one cover manufacturer, supplying top brand original equipment direct to the public.

Over 50 years’ experience

With over 50 years’ experience in this field, Flexicovers has specialized in the design and manufacture of boat covers, bimini tops and marine awnings to top class yacht builders including Sunseeker International, Princess Yachts, Fairline Yachts, Sealine & Williams Performance Tenders. This partnership has enabled us to build a large library of templates, to ensure you get the original intended replacement covers for your boat.

Luxury Covers and Shades

With Flexicovers, you can customise your sailing experience to get the most out of your vessel, safe in the knowledge that you are dealing with an experienced brand who keep customer experience at the heart of everything we do. We deal with private individuals as well as supplying to our extensive dealer network, always working to provide you with the perfect cover solution for your vessel.

Internationally Available Covers

Our covers are available across the globe, offering immense flexibility and convenience to our customers – no matter their location. Not only do we directly supply our covers to luxury UK and Italian boat manufacturers, we are also expanding our supply network of replacement OEM covers worldwide. This has made it more convenient than ever for our customers to get hold of Flexicovers products; it’s now possible for customers to use our world-wide ‘Dealer Network’, spanning the UK, Europe and US, to allow clients to ‘Find a Dealer’ close to home to purchase quality replacement covers

Innovative Product Design

We work closely with boat builders in the design and manufacture of our covers, using CAD technology alongside 3D modelling to ensure a perfect fit. We have an extensive library of CAD templates which allows us to perfectly retro fit boat covers and biminis.

At Flexicovers, we offer a complete design, manufacturing and fitting service for hydraulic and manually operated biminis and awnings, supplying genuine replacement covers for Sunseeker, Princess and Fairline.
