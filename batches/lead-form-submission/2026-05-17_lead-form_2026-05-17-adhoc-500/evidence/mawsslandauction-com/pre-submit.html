Property Info

RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY - AUCTION ANNOUNCEMENT

7.5 ACRES OF HIGH-PROFILE RESIDENTIAL & UNDEVELOPED LAND

Positioned in the thriving neighborhood of The Village of Spring Hill, this 7.5-acre parcel presents an exceptional residential development opportunity. Featuring full utility access, and R-1 zoning, the site is primed for single-family construction in a high-demand market. Its proximity to I-65, schools, and retail centers enhances both convenience and long-term investment potential.

 

 

Appraisal Report Plat Map Environmental Report Phase 1 Report

 

 

Key Features:

  • 7.5 Acres of Prime Residential Land
  • Situated on Mordecai Lane, just off McGregor Avenue in the desirable Spring Hill neighborhood of Mobile, Alabama.
  • Conveniently located near I-65 with easy access to thriving businesses.
  • Formerly a water reservoir site and now prepared for redevelopment, with plans to fill and level the existing basin.
  • Soil and topography rated “adequate for development” with minimal landscaping and clear accessibility.
  • Not located in a flood zone and no observable wetlands, ensuring straightforward construction.
  • Zoned R-1 (Single-Family Residential) by the City of Mobile; ideal for a neighborhood of estate homes or luxury single-family lots.
  • Utilities already available to the site (water, sewer, power, communications).
