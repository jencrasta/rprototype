Luxurious and lasting warmth in your home

Our Purpose

We bring warmth, comfort, and reliability to spaces through our innovative heating solutions. We are driven by a commitment to quality, sustainability and the satisfaction of our customers. We have the most comprehensive warranty of any domestic heater available in New Zealand today. By combining expert craftsmanship with advanced technology, we create products that stand the test of time and enrich lives. Our mission is to be a trusted partner through every season, dedicated to improving the way people experience warmth.

Our Products

We believe that great fires are more than just wood burners—they are an essential part of a home’s character. Each fire is crafted to deliver exceptional performance, reduce environmental impact and meet the diverse needs of our customers. All models meet clean air standards. Built to enhance comfort. Built to last.

Our People

Founded in 1977 on the principle of 'quality first'. We are 100% New Zealand owned. Our people are at the heart of everything we do. Our team is passionate, skilled and dedicated to delivering exceptional products and service. When you join the Yunca family, you are supported by a team that cares deeply about the warmth and wellbeing of everyone in your space.
