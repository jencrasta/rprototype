We Recycle

Soil & Aggregates

Soil & stone is recycled into new products through our sister company, Neal Soil. The materials are treated & processed to create products for the construction industry.

Wood

Wood is graded & segregated: Chipped wood is used for manufacturing boards, sawdust is used in agriculture & wood of the lowest grade is used as fuel in biomass energy facilities.

Waste Electrical & Electronic Equipment

(WEEE) We segregate all electrical items & work with an approved partner to comply with legislation, aiming to lessen the amount of harmful products dumped in landfills

Shredding & Waste Fuel Production

We use machinery to transform these materials into waste fuels. These materials can power energy facilities as a sustainable alternative to fossil fuels.

Paper scraps collected for recycling

Paper fibre waste is segregated & transferred to paper & card mills to be recycled. Recycling paper helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions that contribute to climate change

Collected glass scrap ready for recycling

All glass is recycled and reused. The glass is transferred to UK based plants to be transformed into a wide range of new products, aggregate sub-bases and fibre glass insulation.

Metal scraps ready for recycling at Atlantic Recycling

Our MRF segregates and recovers all types ferrous and non-ferrous metals. These are then delivered to UK based metal recycling companies to produce new reusable products.

Plastics

Plastics are divided according to type - this separation enables us to meet the requirements of plastic recyclers who utilise the different polymers when creating new products.
