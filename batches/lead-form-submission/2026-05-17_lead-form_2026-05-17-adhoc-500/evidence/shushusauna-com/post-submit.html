Timeless Benefits of Sauna Bathing

Spanning from Ottoman hammams to Japanese mushi-buros, each culture has woven the healing powers of heat therapy into its own unique tradition. Saunas stand out for their unmatched therapeutic warmth, offering a myriad of benefits when done regularly:

  • Enhanced immune system function
  • Accelerated cell regeneration
  • Boosted metabolism
  • Natural detoxification of heavy metals and chemicals
  • Support for weight loss, pain relief, skin rejuvenation, and anti-aging
  • Improved circulation and heart health
  • Enhanced muscle recovery and function

Sauna and Cold Plunge Combo Benefits

Often referred to as the ‘Nordic Cycle’ or ‘Viking Bath’, combining a hot sauna with a cold plunge is a fantastic way to maximise the benefits of both:

  • Boosted circulation and cardiovascular health
  • Enhanced metabolism and calorie burning
  • Strengthened immune system
  • Improved muscle recovery and reduced inflammation
  • Increased energy levels and mental alertness
  • Relaxation and stress relief

Our Story

Meet Jari, a Finnish-Canadian carpenter with sauna in his DNA. Coming from a long line of carpenters, he was practically born with a hammer and saw in his hands. Starting when he was only 11 years old, he honed his woodwork skills under the guidance of his father and grandfather, building traditional Finnish saunas in every house they or any of their family members lived in.

Mix grew up in Cape Town and has spent the last decade living in the great North, immersing herself in its sauna culture. Both Mix and Jari have always been in awe of the cultural, spiritual, physical, and emotional richness of sauna bathing.

It wasn’t until a trip to Finland to meet Jari’s new nephew before moving back to Cape Town that Mix and Jari were inspired to bring sauna culture along with its incredible health benefits to Cape Town.

Now, Jari and Mix proudly unveil ShuShu, derived from the Xhosa word for ‘hot,’ symbolising their shared dedication to health, wellness, and community. Join them in experiencing the unmatched therapeutic bliss of Nordic sauna bathing in Cape Town.

Come along for the ride!

FAQ

How it works:
  • The sauna is heated to about 85-105°C, creating the perfect environment for deep seating and relaxation
  • After spending time in the sauna, cool off with a dip in the ocean to activate your circulation and boost your mood
  • This hot-cold method, known as the Nordic Cycle (or Viking Bath), is a time-tested wellness ritual practiced around the world
  • Alternate between sauna and cold plunge at your own pace. Listen to your body, slow down, and enjoy the full sensory experience.
Does my booked time include cool-down breaks and swimming?

Yes. Your reserved time slot (e.g., 30 or 60 minutes, etc) covers your entire session. This includes time spent inside the hot sauna, as well as any time spent outside cooling down, swimming, or resting.

What should I bring for my sauna session?

To make the most of your sauna and cold water immersion session, please pack the following:

  • Proof of booking 
  • Comfortable swimwear
  • Two towels – one to sit on in the sauna, and one for drying off
  • A reusable water bottle, staying hydrated is essential
  • Flip flops or slides for moving around safely
  • Warm clothes for after your session — you’ll thank us later
WHat time should I arrive for my sauna booking?
  • Please arrive 10 minutes before your booking to check in, hear a quick briefing, and get comfortable.We recommend arriving in your swim gear if possible to make things simple.This helps you make the most of your sauna experience.
How can I book a sauna session?

Booking is easy:

  • Use our Booking Page to reserve your spot
  • Or just walk in — we’ll fit you in if there’s space, but bookings take priority
  • Follow @shushusauna on Instagram for the latest updates

Join our WhatsApp group for real-time info and schedule drops

What should I do in the sauna?

Relax and meditate, enjoy community and conversation, or go inside and just feel. Have fun!

There’s no one-size-fits-all when it comes to sauna. You can:

  • Sit in silence and focus on your breath
  • Stretch mindfully or tune into your body
  • Enjoy meaningful conversation with others
  • Gaze out at the ocean through the sauna window
  • Just relax and soak it all in

Whether you’re seeking reflection, connection, or calm, it’s your space to unwind and restore.

Are there any health conditions that make sauna unsuitable?

Sauna use may not be suitable for everyone. Please consult your healthcare provider before attending a session if you are:

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Living with heart conditions, high/low blood pressure, or circulatory issues
  • Managing certain skin conditions or open wounds
  • Prone to fainting or heat sensitivity
  • Under the age of 5

By participating in a sauna or cold plunge session, you acknowledge that you are doing so at your own risk and have assessed your personal health accordingly.

If you’re unsure, please reach out, we’re happy to guide you or help you find the right experience for your needs.

How frequently can I sauna?

The amazing health benefits of sauna bathing increase with regular saunas. In many parts of the world, sauna is a part of daily life, and we recommend that you follow that wisdom.

In many cultures, sauna is a daily wellness ritual and the more consistently you use it, the more benefits you’ll feel.

Whether it’s once a week or multiple times, find your rhythm and let the sauna become a space to de-stress, detox, and reconnect with yourself and others.

What are the health benefits of sauna and cold immersion?

Sauna and cold immersion are a powerful duo, loved for centuries across cultures. Together, they may support:

  • Boost your immune system naturally and support your body’s defences
  • Lower stress levels and ground yourself into inner calm
  • Sleep deeper and wake up feeling refreshed
  • Detoxify from the inside out by flushing heavy metals and toxins
  • Get glowing, rejuvenated skin with accelerated cell renewal
  • Enhance muscle recovery and function after physical activity
  • Improve circulation, heart health, and metabolism to support overall wellbeing

Consistency is key as  the benefits grow over time as part of a regular ritual.
