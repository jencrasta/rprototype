Everything you need to know about Savvy and Ripples™

Savvy is a free AI-powered options trading tracker built by Aculogic Solutions. It automatically extracts trade data from screenshots using Quick Capture, tracks P&L and win rates, and provides advanced analytics — especially for traders running the wheel strategy.
Ripples™ is a market intelligence feature inside Savvy that helps options traders read GEX (Gamma Exposure) walls, map banker vs retail open interest and volume flow, and identify key price levels for entries and exits. Smart money leaves a wake — Ripples™ lets you read it.
A Gamma Wall (or GEX wall) is a price level where market makers have significant gamma exposure and are forced to hedge by buying or selling the underlying. These levels act as magnetic price zones the market gravitates toward or gets repelled from. Ripples™ visualises Gamma Walls in real time so you know exactly where to place your entries and exits.
In Ripples™, Banker flow refers to large institutional whale movements — the biggest players in the market. Hot Money represents active hedge funds and sophisticated traders (dolphins and sharks) making rapid directional bets. Retail flow represents individual small traders (small fish). By tracking all three, you can see who is really driving price and trade with conviction.
Quick Capture is an AI-powered feature that lets you upload a screenshot of your trades — from any broker — and automatically extracts all trade details including ticker, strike, expiry, premium, and quantity. No manual data entry required.

It tracks cash-secured puts and covered calls, shows monthly premium income, displays open positions sorted by days to expiry (DTE), tracks rolled options history, and with Ripples™ shows GEX walls and banker vs retail flow so you know exactly where to sell for consistent income.
