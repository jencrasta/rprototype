How it works

Agents that connect, build, and ship through Brevo

Three steps from raw e-commerce data to compliant, multi-channel campaigns.

1. Connect

Plug in your Brevo account and your data sources: Shopify, Stripe, Magento, HubSpot, and more. Declarative field maps replace fragile ETL scripts.

2. Build

Specialist agents take over: Sync Operator pulls data, Segment Builder defines audiences, Engagement Designer drafts campaigns. All routed through Brevo's engagement engine.

3. Approve & ship

The Compliance Reviewer gates every send against consent, budget, and regulatory rules. You approve above your thresholds, Brevo handles delivery, and the audit log captures every decision.

Connect 100+ sources to Brevo

The Sync Operator agent pulls contacts, orders, products, and events from Shopify, Stripe, Magento, and 100+ other systems into Brevo in real time. Declarative field maps replace fragile ETL scripts, no code to maintain as schemas evolve.

  • 100+ pre-built connectors (Shopify, Stripe, Magento, more)
  • Declarative field maps, no ETL code required
  • Real-time webhooks + resumable batch cursors
  • Bi-directional sync with Brevo contacts and events

Audiences that refresh themselves

The Segment Builder agent defines rule-based audiences across your Brevo contacts, "purchased in last 30 days AND not engaged in 90 days", and keeps them current automatically as new data streams in. No more stale CSV exports.

  • Rule-based segments with live refresh
  • RFM, LTV, and engagement recency built in
  • Materialized member counts per segment
  • Reusable across campaigns and workflows

Multi-channel campaigns, drafted for you

The Engagement Designer agent proposes audience, creative, send timing, and channel for every campaign. Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and mobile wallet, all orchestrated through Brevo with 99% transactional delivery.

  • Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and mobile wallet via Brevo
  • AI-drafted creative with send-time optimization
  • Goal → Plan → Task hierarchy mirrors how marketers think
  • 99% transactional delivery (Brevo SMTP relay)

Every send passes governance gates

The Compliance Reviewer agent verifies consent logs, CAN-SPAM / GDPR / TCPA rules, and budget caps before any campaign leaves Brevo. Human approval is required above the thresholds you set, and every decision lands in an immutable audit log.

  • CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and TCPA checks pre-send
  • Human approval gates on spend and audience size
  • Monthly budget caps per workspace, agent, and campaign
  • Immutable audit log with revenue attribution

Frequently Asked Questions

Tajo is your agentic marketing team for Brevo. A Sync Operator pulls data from 100+ e-commerce and CRM sources into Brevo, a Segment Builder defines live-refreshing audiences, an Engagement Designer drafts email / SMS / WhatsApp / wallet campaigns, and a Compliance Reviewer gates every send against consent, budget, and regulatory rules.
Four specialist agents cover the full campaign lifecycle on Brevo. (1) Sync Operator, connects Shopify, Stripe, Magento, HubSpot, and 100+ other systems via declarative field maps. (2) Segment Builder, creates audiences from RFM, LTV, and engagement rules that refresh as data flows in. (3) Engagement Designer, proposes audience, creative, channel, and send time for each campaign. (4) Compliance Reviewer, verifies CAN-SPAM / GDPR / TCPA, checks budget caps, and escalates to a human when you've configured an approval threshold.
You set approval thresholds, for example, "every send over 10,000 contacts" or "every SMS campaign". Before any dispatch, the Compliance Reviewer checks consent records, audience size, and regulatory requirements, then routes to your human approver when a rule fires. Every decision, agent and human, is written to an immutable audit log that you can export for regulators.
Over 100 sources across e-commerce (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, Stripe), CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho), marketing (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Iterable), and operations (Slack, Airtable, Zapier, Notion, Zendesk). Connectors are declarative, no ETL code for your team to maintain.
Every workspace has a monthly budget with per-agent and per-campaign sub-limits. Warning thresholds trigger alerts at configurable percentages; hard stops block sends once caps are reached. Revenue is attributed back to each campaign through Brevo's tracking, so you see ROI per agent and per plan, not just aggregate spend.
