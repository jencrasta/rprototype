Our Services: Screen Printing and Embroidery in Grand Rapids, MI

Printed Marketplace is a design and print shop proudly serving Grand Rapids and our local Michigan community. We specialize in customized apparel including logo screen printing and embroidery services. We pride ourselves on high quality printing and services that are both fast and reliable—offering you a much better product than most other custom print companies at an unbeatable price. Whether you need screen printing, embroidery, or both for your custom order, we're your one-stop shop. As a screen printing company in Grand Rapids, we offer local screen printing services to our local West Michigan community, but also throughout the United States. Getting your design printed from anywhere in the States is easy with our online designer!
