An unrivalled network

Headquartered in Australia and with regional offices in South East Asia, our physical clubs and world-class digital products give us unparalleled reach to spread health and happiness across Asia Pacific.

Through our powerful portfolio of globally recognised brands, we help over 585k members of 330+ clubs to reap the many rewards of a healthy, active lifestyle.​
