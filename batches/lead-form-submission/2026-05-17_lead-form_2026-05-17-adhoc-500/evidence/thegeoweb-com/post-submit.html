Why Beauty & Wellness?

Rising Consumption, Everyday Use

Beauty & wellness are now daily habits, not occasional treats—Indians are using multiple skincare, haircare, makeup and supplement products every single day, pushing the personal care market towards double-digit annual growth.

Massive Consumer Shift to ‘Wellness First’

Post-Covid, customers actively look for clean, science-backed, skin-safe and wellness-led products—gut health, sleep, stress, skin barrier, hormone balance. This shift from vanity to well-being has unlocked huge demand for specialised D2C brands.

Market Gap for Focused, Premium Brands

Legacy FMCG players still sell mass products; there’s a clear gap for niche, story-driven brands (acne-only, scalp-only, underarm care, teen skin, intimate wellness, nutricosmetics) with better education, UX and positioning.

High Margins & Long-Term Brand Building

Beauty & wellness products combine healthy gross margins with strong repeat purchase—once a routine works, customers buy again and again. That makes this category perfect for long-term brand compounding, higher LTV and premium positioning.
