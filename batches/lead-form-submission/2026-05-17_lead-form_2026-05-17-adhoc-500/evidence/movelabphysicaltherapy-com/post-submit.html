At MoveLab Physical Therapy & Sports Performance in Erie, Colorado, we’re redefining what recovery and performance look like. Every session is a full hour with a dedicated physical therapist, designed to get you out of pain faster, build lasting strength, and help you exceed your goals.

Our Services

Beyond Pain Relief

Progressive Pain-to-Performance Solutions

Physical Therapy

One-on-one, one-hour sessions focused on relieving pain, restoring mobility, and getting you back to the activities you love—faster.

Personal Training

Targeted strength, conditioning, and movement training to improve performance, prevent injury, and help you excel in your sport or active lifestyle.

Spine Care

Diagnosis and treatment planning of ever-present, complex spine conditions. Deploying hands on dry needling, spinal manipulation and therapeutic exercises

Orthopedic Care

Upper extremity (shoulder, elbow and wrist) and lower extremity (hip, knee and foot/ankle) care. Utilizizing advanced manual therapy techniques to restore mechanics and build strength

Sports Medicine

Rehabilitative and post-operative care for orthopedic injuries.

Movement Examination

Are you moving well? Our individualized movement exams combine data with the highest evidence to KEEP you moving well and optimize your performance.
