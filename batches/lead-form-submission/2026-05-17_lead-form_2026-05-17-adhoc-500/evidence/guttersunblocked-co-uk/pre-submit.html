Gutter cleaning across Nottingham and the East Midlands

Gutters Unblocked provides professional gutter cleaning, blocked downpipe clearance and gutter repairs for homes, landlords, letting agents and commercial properties across Nottingham and the wider East Midlands.

We help prevent overflowing gutters, damp patches, blocked rainwater systems and costly water damage by clearing gutters safely from the ground wherever possible.

Our work is guaranteed

All gutter cleaning and repair work is carried out professionally and with care. If an issue returns shortly after our visit due to the workmanship of the clean or repair, we will return and put it right.

Commercial Gutter Maintenance

Planned gutter cleaning for commercial properties

We provide planned gutter cleaning and rainwater system maintenance for commercial properties, landlords, letting agents, factories, offices, schools, retail units and property managers.

Regular gutter maintenance helps reduce emergency call outs, internal leaks, overflowing gutters, blocked downpipes and water damage during heavy rain.

We can provide one off visits or scheduled maintenance depending on the property and risk level.
