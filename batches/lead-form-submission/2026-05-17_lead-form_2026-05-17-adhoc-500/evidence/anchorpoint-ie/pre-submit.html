Modern life keeps us constantly switched on…

Anchor Point is a Bio Energy practice that focuses on helping people slow down, recharge, and reconnect with themselves.

My Story

I created Anchor Point from personal experience.

After struggling with panic attacks and feeling constantly stuck in fight-or-flight, Bio Energy was what genuinely helped me feel calmer, more balanced and more connected to myself again.

Through Bio Energy, I began to understand how deeply stress, emotional experiences and modern life can affect the body, and how powerful the body can be when given the space and support to properly rebalance itself.

What is Bio Energy?

Bio Energy is a non-invasive practice based on working with the body’s natural life-force energy and electromagnetic field, often referred to as the human energy field or aura. Sessions involve working with the hands on and around the body, particularly around chakra points and areas such as the head, heart and spine.

The practice is based on how stress, trauma and life experiences can affect the flow of energy throughout the body over time. Bio Energy aims to help restore balance within the body’s energy systems and support the body’s natural ability to rebalance itself.

Frequently asked questions

When do bookings open for 1-1 sessions?

Bookings will open on June 5th to those on the waitlist via email. Bookings will open to the public from June 9th.

What issues can Bio Energy treat?

People seek out bio-energy sessions for many different reasons including stress, anxiety, burnout, low mood, emotional overwhelm, sleep issues, low energy, gut-related issues, skin conditions, chronic tension and feeling mentally or physically out of balance. Some people also explore bio-energy alongside other supports for issues such as autoimmune conditions, tinnitus, fertility challenges and neurodivergent conditions. Bio-energy focuses on supporting the body’s overall balance and natural ability to regulate and restore itself rather than targeting one specific condition alone.

Where are sessions based?

Anchor Point will initially be based in Artane, Dublin 5, with the exact studio address and booking details being released before launch in June 2026.

How long do sessions last?

Sessions are approximately 1 hour long. For the greatest benefit, it is generally recommended to have 3 sessions over 3 consecutive days where possible. However, sessions can also be spread out over a timeframe that best suits you and your schedule.
