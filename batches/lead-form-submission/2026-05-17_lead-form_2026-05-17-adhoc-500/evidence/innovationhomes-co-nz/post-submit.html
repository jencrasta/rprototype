FAQs about Innovation Homes

What makes Innovation Homes different?

By combining prefabricated components, energy‑smart design, and renewable technology, Innovation Homes has created a new era in home design for the conscious consumer. We’ve simplified the process with a home that has the sustainable and low‑maintenance features you want, plus many other premium features you didn’t even know you needed. When building with us, you’ll enjoy a fast and stress-free building process.

What type of construction system do you use?

We use a precision-built construction approach that combines off-site manufacturing with skilled on-site building to deliver homes that are faster to build, consistently finished, and designed to perform better over time.

Do I need to own land before I talk to you?

Not at all. Whether you already own land or are still searching, we can guide you through design, consents, and site assessment. Our House & Land Packages are ready to go in Northland.

How long does it take to build a home?

Most builds are completed in 4–6 months, with the full process (design, consents, and construction) taking 6–8 months. Once confirmed, you’ll receive a fixed-price contract with no hidden extras.

Can I customise a design?

Absolutely. Choose a standard design or work with us to create a fully custom home tailored to your lifestyle, finishes, and location.

What’s included in a build by Innovation Homes?

Every build by Innovation Homes includes:

  • High-quality materials meeting NZ Building Code standards
  • Light-filled living areas with raked ceilings
  • Complimentary solar package, valued at over $12,000
  • Full project management from concept to keys
  • Council consents and documentation handled
  • 10-Year Halo Guarantee
  • Optional upgrades available to personalise your home
Why should I choose Innovation Homes?

Because you deserve a home that’s modern, efficient, and built to last. Our smarter building methods, solar inclusions, fixed pricing, and 10-Year Halo Guarantee mean your home is sustainable, stress-free, and future-ready.

About Us

We’re Nicole and Scott, the team behind Innovation Homes.

We live and build right here in Northland, so we understand the land, the lifestyle, and what makes a home work in this part of the world. Through our background with Hargood Builders, we’ve spent years helping people create spaces that feel right; not just on day one, but for years to come.

Nicole works alongside you from the start, helping shape your ideas into a home that suits how you actually live. She’s all about making the process feel easy and personal, no confusion, no pressure, just good communication and thoughtful design.

Scott leads the construction side, bringing everything together. He’s practical, detail-focused, and takes real pride in doing things properly. Known for his calm, solutions-driven mindset and if something needs figuring out, he’ll find the best way to do it, making sure it’s done right.

We don’t do standard plans. Every home is designed for you, your section, and your way of living. We keep things simple, honest, and well thought out—so at the end of it, you’ve got a home that just feels like yours.
