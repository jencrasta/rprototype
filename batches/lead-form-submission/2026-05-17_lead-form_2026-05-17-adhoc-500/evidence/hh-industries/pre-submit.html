Industrialising Africa
One Layer At A Time

HH Industries provides advanced manufacturing and 3D printing (Additive Manufacturing) services for various industries in Africa. We innovate to offer realistic and sustainable solutions for the unique challenges faced within the continent. Our wide variety of services and offerings are complimented through years of hands-on experience.

Part Printing

Together, we bring your idea to reality. Reach out to us with your part ideas or designs and we will walk with you to make it a reality in the most cost effective way.

R&D

We aim to perform relevant R&D - based on real-life applications, comprehensive technologies, and materials - accessible and applicable to key industries. We innovate to improve competitiveness.

Turnkey Facilities

HH Industries offers modular, plug-and-play turnkey facilities for metal additive manufacturing, designed for rapid deployment and self-sufficient operation in Africa’s most challenging environments.

Training

If you have the passion and willingness to learn something new, we can guide you on your journey to master additive manufacturing. From directors to engineers we have training suited for anyone.
