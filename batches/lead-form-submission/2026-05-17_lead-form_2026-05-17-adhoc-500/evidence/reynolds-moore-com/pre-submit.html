What is functional Safety?

Functional Safety for Advanced Automation

Functional safety is about making sure systems work as they should — and handle failures safely when things go wrong. Functional safety engineering is how we design, test, and validate those systems, helping teams build safe solutions without holding back innovation.

We provide functional safety engineering to drive and support safe innovation.

Partnering with innovation leaders through:

  • Research & Development

  • Safety Concept & Strategy

  • System Design & Development

  • Testing & Validation

  • Safety Case & Certification

  • Compliance & Support
