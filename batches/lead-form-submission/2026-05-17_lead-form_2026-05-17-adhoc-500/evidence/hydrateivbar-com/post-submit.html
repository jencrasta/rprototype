For appointments after hours, please inquire with your local spa. Standard IV appointments must be booked one hour before close, and NAD+ IV appointments must be booked four hours before close. We take injection appointments up until 15 minutes prior to close. Service offerings and protocols may vary by spa. Please note that we charge a $25 fee for late cancellations or no-shows. Clients have up to two hours prior to their appointment to cancel; if extenuating circumstances have occurred, please call the spa you’re booked at.
