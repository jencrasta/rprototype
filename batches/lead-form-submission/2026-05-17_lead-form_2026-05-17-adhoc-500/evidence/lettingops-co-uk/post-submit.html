The Problem

Traditional letting agents charge substantial fees but often provide inconsistent operational support. Managing maintenance yourself is time-consuming and reactive.

LettingOps focuses specifically on maintenance coordination and operational efficiency — giving independent landlords a structured, transparent service without requiring full agency involvement.

Core Services

What LettingOps provides

Structured operational coordination for your rental property — from first report to resolved issue.

Maintenance Coordination

We manage tenant-reported maintenance issues end-to-end and coordinate contractors to resolution.

Issue Triage

Every issue is assessed and categorised: emergency, urgent, or routine — ensuring appropriate response times.

Contractor Management

We coordinate trusted independent contractors, schedule visits, and monitor completion of works.

Landlord Reporting

Clear, structured updates on issue progress and outcomes — so you're always informed without being overwhelmed.

Property Inspections

Bi-annual inspections with structured photographic reports — available on Managed and Complete plans.

Approval Controls

Each landlord sets their own repair threshold. We act within it — and always consult you above it.
