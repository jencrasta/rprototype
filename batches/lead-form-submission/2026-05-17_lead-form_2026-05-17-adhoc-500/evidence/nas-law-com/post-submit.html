Building relationships. Focusing on results A consultation that delivers Keeping your best interest in mind .

NASHEF, AUN, SHABAN (NAS) is a boutique law firm and a legal partner for companies, corporations, investors and entrepreneurs, providing legal counsel grounded in professional excellence, strategic thinking, and a comprehensive understanding of the intersection between law, regulation, and business. The firm advises clients operating in dynamic and complex environments, with an uncompromising commitment to the highest professional standards, personalized service, and a clear focus on creating long-term business value.

PRACTICE AREAS

  • Commercial & Corporate Law
  • Construction & Zoning Law
  • Commercial Litigation
  • Dissolution of Real Estate Partnerships
  • Startups, Hi-Tech & Venture Capital
  • Employment & Labor Law
  • Infrastructures & Municipal Corporations
  • Municipal Property Tax
  • Tax Law
  • Private & Commercial Real Estate
  • Intellectual Property
  • Non-Profit Organizations

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At NAS, we believe in building a trust relationship and professional cooperation with our clients, standing by their side in promoting their growth and being available and involved every step of the way. This is because we understand that the basis of every wise business and legal decision is the understanding of all those involved in making it.

Our attorneys hold a strategic and creative approach that stems from a commitment to look at the long term with our clients and move their business towards success. We also pride ourselves on providing accessible and prompt communication as we realize this being key in today’s rapid pace of life.

On top of all, our attorneys strive to create best solutions tailored to our clients’ needs irrespective of their size and profile.
