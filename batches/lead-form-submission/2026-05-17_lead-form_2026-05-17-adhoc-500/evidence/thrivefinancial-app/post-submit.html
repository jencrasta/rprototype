Built For Scrutiny

A stronger record of how
the numbers were organized.

Thrive does not keep a hosted case-data database. Matter data lives locally in the browser until you choose to export, analyze, or share it, and the app preserves the provenance of the files it uses.

For spouses, attorneys, and forensic accountants evaluating a divorce forensic accounting app, this local-first design supports stronger E-E-A-T and trust signals around sensitive financial records.

  • Case state stored locally in IndexedDB on the device
  • `.thrive` case exports include metadata, timestamp, and SHA-256 integrity hash
  • Source files retain stored hashes for downstream provenance
  • Original-file download and certificate workflows stay tied to the parser record
  • Audit-log PDF export documents key case actions and AI workflows
  • Users control when data leaves the device through AI analysis or export actions

Frequently Asked

Questions teams ask before trusting a financial case file.

Clear answers for spouses, attorneys, and forensic accountants evaluating divorce financial analysis software.

Documents

What can Thrive Financial analyze?

Bank and credit card statements, financial affidavits, tax returns, Cash App or P2P histories, business income statements, balance sheets, and general ledgers.

Storage

Where is the case data stored?

The live matter file is stored locally in browser IndexedDB. Users decide when data leaves the device through AI analysis, `.thrive` backups, PDFs, spreadsheets, or original-file downloads.

Teams

Who is it built for?

The current app supports spouses, attorneys, and forensic accountants, with guided novice flows, advanced workflows, discovery tracking, and export features for litigation prep.

Use Limits

Is Thrive Financial legal advice?

No. It is a financial-analysis and case-organization platform. AI findings should still be reviewed against the original source files and applied with attorney strategy in the relevant jurisdiction.
