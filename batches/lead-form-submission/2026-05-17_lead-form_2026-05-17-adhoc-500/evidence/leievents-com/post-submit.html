Welcome to Lei Events!

At Lei Events, we love bringing people together to celebrate life’s special moments. Co-founded by Malie Moran and Maleko McDonnell, our team is behind Honolulu’s Le Diner en Blanc and other memorable gatherings around the islands. We offer event planning, consulting, and creative marketing support that’s tailored to you. With a laid-back island feel and a thoughtful approach to every detail, we’ll help turn your ideas into an event that feels fun, welcoming, and uniquely yours.
