Who We Serve

Everyone Deserves a
Path Forward

DoConnect opens doors for individuals across our Westchester communities — especially those who need it most. Our programs are designed for veterans, justice-impacted individuals, underserved and/or marginalized communities, and anyone ready to take the next step toward meaningful employment.

Veterans transitioning to civilian careers
Justice-impacted individuals rebuilding their futures

Anyone ready to enter or re-enter the workforce

Our Programs

Training That Leads to Real Careers

Industry-recognized certifications. Hands-on instruction. Job placement support. Everything you need to succeed.

42 Hours

Construction Site Safety

Earn OSHA 30, Construction Site Safety, and Flagger certifications. Includes classroom instruction, work equipment, and job placement support.

  • OSHA 30 Certification
  • Flagger Training
  • Work Equipment Provided
  • Job Placement Support
25 Hours

Security Guard Training

Complete NYS-required 8-hour Pre-Assignment and 16-hour On-The-Job Training, plus Fire Guard test prep. Includes background check and registration fees.

  • NYS Certified Program
  • Fire Guard Test Prep
  • Background Check Included
  • Registration Fees Covered
Flexible

Security Guard Renewal

Two 8-hour renewal courses meeting NYS Department of Criminal Justice requirements. Fingerprinting and background checks included.

  • NYS Compliant
  • Fingerprinting Included
  • Background Check Included
  • Stay Certified
