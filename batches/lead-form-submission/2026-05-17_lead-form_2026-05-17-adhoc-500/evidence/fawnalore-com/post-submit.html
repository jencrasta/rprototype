Enter The World

Some games you play.

Fawnalore you live.

A complete gaming universe — rich lore, an episodic story told through voice-acted motion comics, and a deep board game system that rewards every kind of player. Easy to learn. Built to last. Open the box tonight.

The Experience

The story begins before you touch the board.

Every episode. Three steps. Your pace, your way.

Watch or Listen

A fully voice-acted motion comic on YouTube or your favorite podcast app. Watch the episode for the full visual experience, or listen on your commute. Every Act releases all at once — no waiting, no weekly schedule unless you want one.

Prepare

The world you just experienced becomes the board in front of you. Every episode has a dedicated setup page on our site — map layout, starting conditions, and objectives — free and open to anyone. Scan a QR code from your box and you're ready to play.

Play

The outcome is yours to decide. Explore the board, battle the Consumed, and fight to complete the episode objective. New areas and story beats unlock mid-game through scannable campaign tiles — the world literally expands as you play. The story told you what happened. The game lets you determine what happens next.

About The World

THREE WAYS TO PLAY

One system. Every kind of game night.

Campaign

A lore-rich episodic adventure that's easy to pick up and impossible to put down. Watch or listen to the episode first, then bring that world to the table. All 10 episodes are available from day one — play at your own pace, on your own terms.

PvP

Head-to-head combat with real strategic depth. Build your deck, read your opponent, and fight for dominance on a board that changes every match. Fast enough for a game night. Deep enough to keep coming back.

Sandbox

The rules are yours to bend. Build custom maps, invent new modes, write your own episodes. The system is designed to be taken apart and rebuilt — a creative platform as much as a game.
