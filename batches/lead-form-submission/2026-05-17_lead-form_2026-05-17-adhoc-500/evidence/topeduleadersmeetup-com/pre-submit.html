Our Mission

Where Education
Leaders Come to
Shape What's Next

The Global Education Conference is a premier platform gathering the most renowned educationists, EdTech innovators, policymakers, and researchers to address the most critical challenges in education worldwide.

We believe education is the fundamental driver of human progress. Our three-city annual series exists to build meaningful connections, surface transformative ideas, and inspire the systemic change our world urgently needs.

Key Themes &
Agenda Topics

Across all three editions, our agenda is designed to challenge, inspire, and equip every attendee with ideas they can act on.

01
AI & the Future of Teaching

How artificial intelligence is reshaping the classroom, personalizing learning, and redefining the role of educators globally.

02
Education Policy & Systemic Reform

Policymakers and institutional leaders share strategies for systemic change that bridges the gap between vision and practice.

03
Inclusive & Equitable Learning

Designing education systems that leave no learner behind — across ability, geography, language, and socioeconomic status.

04
EdTech Innovation & Investment

The latest in learning technology — from platforms to assessment tools — and the investment landscape shaping EdTech's future.

05
Future Skills & Workforce Readiness

Preparing students and institutions for the rapidly evolving demands of global workforces, lifelong learning, and new economies.

06
Research, Data & Evidence in Education

Rigorous research and data-driven insights that challenge assumptions and fuel education reform grounded in evidence.
