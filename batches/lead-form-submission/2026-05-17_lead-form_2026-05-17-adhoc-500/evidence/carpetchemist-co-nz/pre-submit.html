About Carpet Chemist

Over a decade ago, in the heart of New Zealand, Carpet Chemist was created with a clear mission:

To offer Kiwis a viable and cost-effective alternative to carpet replacement. We are a small business with strong family values that is fast becoming an industry leader in professional carpet cleaning services and stain solutions across NZ.  As a trusted carpet cleaning company that values customer service, our commitment is to provide true quality and value every time.
