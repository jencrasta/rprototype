Darren Bell, CEO of dB SoundVision

Darren Bell, founder and CEO of dB SoundVision, is an accomplished location sound and ADR mixer with over a decade of professional experience in the Los Angeles film and television industry. A proud member of IATSE Local 695 for more then 15 years, Darren has become a household name in the sound mixer community, trusted by producers and directors across genres.

He is best known for his work on the award-winning documentary The 13th, directed by Ava DuVernay, and has built a diverse portfolio across episodic television, feature films, documentaries, commercials, and music projects. His television credits include hit series such as Yellowstone, Bones, Castle, The Last Ship, Lucifer, 9-1-1, and Stumptown.

With a veteran’s expertise and a commitment to excellence, Darren brings a tailored sound approach to every production, ensuring clarity, precision, and creativity. Whether on set or in post, he has the skill and experience to deliver broadcast-ready sound that elevates storytelling.
