About Us

At Wiltshire Windows, we provide quality and bespoke double and triple glazed products throughout Wiltshire, Swindon and surrounding areas. Whether you’re looking for windows, doors, conservatories, secondary glazing or porches, we’re here to help.

We make it our aim to help customers enhance and transform their homes using our tailored advice, customer support and non-pressurised sales approach. Being a trusted family run business, customer satisfaction and high quality products are always our priorities.
