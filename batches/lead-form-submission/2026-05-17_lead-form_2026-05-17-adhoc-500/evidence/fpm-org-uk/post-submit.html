Join FPM

We provide a collective voice for our 1,600 members who are striving to advance the research and development of new medicines to help prevent and overcome diseases that impact on the lives of patients worldwide. They are all medically-qualified and employed within the pharmaceutical industry, research organisations, drug regulatory authorities – or working as independent consultants.  The strength of our discipline and of FPM is that our members work across a diverse range of careers; from front line clinical trials, to pharmaceutical marketing and medicines regulation.

Membership is open to medically qualified doctors who work in, or have a professional interest in, pharmaceutical medicine.  We have six different types of membership listed below and if you are new to FPM, you may want to look at Affiliate and Membership (MFPM) first.

Contact us on membership@fpm.org.uk if you have any questions about becoming a member.

Affiliate membership


Apply today

Open to any registered medical practitioner who works in, or can demonstrate a professional interest and connection with, pharmaceutical medicine.

Find out more

Membership (MFPM)


Apply today

Open to a registered medical pracitioner who has a minimum of two years’ experience in a pharmaceutical medical role and holds a post graduate qualification approved for MFPM.

Find out more

Fellowship (FFPM)


Applications are closed for 2026

Eligibility is based on experience and seniority in the profession; it is not dependent on time served as a Member (MFPM). Fellowship recognises achievement at a high level in the profession and confers peer recognition.

Find out more

Associate membership


For PMST students only

Applies to those enrolled on the Pharmaceutical Medicine Specialty Training (PMST) programme. You will automatically be enrolled as an Associate member as part of your application and enrolment onto the PMST programme.

Find out more about PMST

Membership by Distinction (MFPM(Dis))


Applications are closed for 2026

This is an exceptional route to Membership for individuals who have made a significant contribution beyond the normal day-to-day practice of pharmaceutical medicine. Nominations are opened annually in January.

Find out if you are eligible to nominate

Honorary Fellowship and Membership


Applications are closed for 2026

Honorary awards recognise the recipient’s eminence within their own field and their outstanding contribution to pharmaceutical medicine. Honorary Fellows and Members need not be medically qualified. Nominations are opened annually in January.

Find out if you are eligible to nominate
