Choosing the right size

ReSheyP comes in three sizes to accommodate all anatomies.  Please use the guide below to select your correct fit.

  1. Measure the circumference (distance around the shaft) of your penis in the flaccid state.

    a. Wrap a string or flexible material loosely around the center of the penile shaft. 

    b. Check the length of the string by placing it on a ruler. For the best accuracy, we recommend measuring in centimeters.

  2. Compare this measurement to the numbers below and select the size to the right.

    If you have questions about sizing, please contact us at the email or phone number below and we’ll be happy to help answer any questions you have.
