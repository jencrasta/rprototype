Questions about Northwestern Mutual? We've got answers.

Our pricing for insurance and fees for investing are straightforward, and we'll always be upfront about them. What you pay monthly (or yearly) for our insurance solutions is determined by things like the amount of coverage you need and your age. For an advisory or private wealth management investment account, you'll pay a quarterly fee. The fee is based on the value of the assets in your account. Our brokerage accounts and services work on a commission for each asset purchased.
