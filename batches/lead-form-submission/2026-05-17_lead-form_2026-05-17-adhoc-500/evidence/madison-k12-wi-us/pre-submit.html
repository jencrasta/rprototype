Join Us to Shape the Future of Our Schools

MMSD invites families, students, staff and community members to join us for upcoming “Building for the Future” input sessions.

Your voice matters to help shape future enrollment and determine school attendance boundaries. No decisions have been made — this is your opportunity to help guide the future and strengthen MMSD as a destination school district.

Come early to our in-person sessions to connect with others and enjoy dinner before the
presentation begins. Food and refreshments will be available 30 minutes prior to each session.

At these sessions, you will:

  • Learn why MMSD is conducting a boundary and attendance area review
  • Explore enrollment trends, facility use and growth projections
  • Gain a clear understanding of the process and timeline
  • Ask questions and share your perspectives
  • View draft boundary scenarios for feedback

In-Person Events
Tuesday, May 19 | 3:30–5:30 p.m., UW South Madison Partnership, 2238 S. Park St.
Wednesday, May 20 | 5:30–7:30 p.m., Warner Park Community Recreation Center, 1625 Northport Dr.
Thursday, May 21 | 5-7 p.m., CENTRO, 2403 Cypress Way (Presentation in Spanish)
