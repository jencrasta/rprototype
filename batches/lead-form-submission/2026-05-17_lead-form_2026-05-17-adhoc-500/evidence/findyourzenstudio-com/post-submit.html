Meet the Maker

Yema has always lived at the intersection of the inner and the outer, the reflective and the tangible. As a life coach and writer with a spiritual edge, she has spent years helping people find clarity, voice, and direction.

As a maker, she channels that same intention into every bar of soap, every fragrance, and every candle she crafts by hand in London.

Skin Confidential is not merely a metaphor. It is a way of moving through the world, tending, nurturing, and paying attention. It is the philosophy behind everything here.

Read more about the work

People come to me when they are tired of feeling overwhelmed, unseen, dry-skinned, creatively blocked, spiritually disconnected, or unsure how to bring their ideas into the world.They stay because the work feels calm, honest, beautiful, and deeply human.
