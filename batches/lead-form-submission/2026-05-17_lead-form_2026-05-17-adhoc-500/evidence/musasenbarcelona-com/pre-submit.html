Our workshops

Welcome to Musas, your creative sanctuary

A space to connect with your most authentic self through art.

At Musas, we invite you to explore your creativity with no pressure, no judgment, and an open heart. Our in-person workshops—featuring collage, mixed media, and astrology—are intimate gatherings where art, intuition, and inspiration intertwine to create truly unique moments.

No prior experience is needed—just a willingness to play, express yourself, and let go. Each session is a break from the everyday noise, a chance to reconnect with yourself and with other creative souls.

This is your sanctuary, your creative ritual, your space for expression.

Musas for Teams

Creative workshops to connect, feel good, and flow as a team

We offer artistic experiences designed for companies, coworking spaces, and groups looking to strengthen bonds, break out of routine, and awaken their creativity.

Tailor-made workshops using collage and intuitive art techniques—perfect for team-building days, incentives, or special celebrations.
All materials are included, along with a cozy space and optional celebratory toast to end the experience with heart.
