Maringouin

(pronounced mahr-in-gwin)

On the Mississippi Gulf Coast, the air buzzes with anticipation long before the first plate glides across the table.

Take a seat. You can feel it, can’t you? Something vibrant, unpredictable, and daring is about to happen.

Locals have a word for it — maringouin. Mosquito.

Because the Gulf Coast has never been one thing. It’s layered. Influenced. Fluttering with contradiction. It’s unpredictable in the best way possible.

At Maringouin, the menu bends and blends international cultures with community, local with boundless influences, to create a modern dining experience that hums and spins through every course.

Southern soul buzzing with global bite

Maringouin is led by Executive Chef Lauren Joffrion and Sous Chef Brennan Edmonds
