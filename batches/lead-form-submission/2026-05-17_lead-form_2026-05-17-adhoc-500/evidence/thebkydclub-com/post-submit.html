About The Backyard Club

The Backyard Club (TBC) is a premium indoor pickleball and social club launching in 2026 at 1800 Steeles Ave. W., Vaughan. Our mission is to create a welcoming, elevated environment where sport, wellness, and community come together.

  • Pickleball, Basketball, Golf
  • Food & Drinks
  • Events
  • Programming & Clinics
  • Training & Academy
  • Casual & Social
  • All in Your Backyard

The Backyard Club is our family's vision to share our backyard with the Toronto and GTA community.

As a family-built business, we know great clubs are shaped over time. We are committed to listening closely, learning continuously, and thoughtfully improving every step of the way.

We are grateful for the opportunity to bring this vision to life, and we cannot wait to welcome you. This is the backyard we've always dreamed of…
