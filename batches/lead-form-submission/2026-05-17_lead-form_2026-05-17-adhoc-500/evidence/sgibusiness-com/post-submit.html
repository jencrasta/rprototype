Love & Like yourself journal

You’ve built the business. You show up every day. But something still feels off.

The Love & Like Yourself Journal is a faith-rooted guide designed for female business owners who are ready to discover their worth, heal what’s holding them back, and step boldly into who God created them to be.

Working through 12 sections — Physically, Mentally, Spiritually, Emotionally, Financially, Spiritual Gifts, Healing From Your Past, Forgiveness, God’s Big Vision, and becoming You 10.0 — this journal doesn’t just ask you to write your feelings. It asks you to seek God about every area of your life.
