How we work with you

Our commitment to professional asbestos and hazardous waste removal involves a meticulous process, including:

 

Planning

We undertake a site visit to create a specific plan for your site. This method means we identify all asbestos materials present and aspects required to complete your job on time and within your budget. A project-specific health and safety plan is created in the form of an ARCP (asbestos removal control plan) to ensure all steps of the asbestos removal process are documented and followed.

Asbestos Removal

We take full control of the site and limit access to trained professionals only. The team at LAS set up all safety precautions and measures to carefully contain and remove all hazardous waste from your site. All asbestos waste is carefully contained as per legislative requirements and disposed of at a licenced facility in Auckland for your safety.

Rest Easy

Before our final handover, we engage a third-party clearance inspection to ensure all aspects of the job have been completed correctly. You can rest easy knowing you have a safe site ready for ongoing construction or renovation–safe for your builders and secure for your family for years to come.
