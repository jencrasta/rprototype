FAQs

Got questions? 

  • I haven't done a deal yet, is this for me?

    Absolutely.  What comes first - the deal or the investor?  If you know you need investment in your property business,  you can't do one without the other.  Relationships take time to build, so don't leave this part of your marketing strategy to the last minute.  

  • What happens on the 1:1 call?

    We will meet via Google Meet for a virtual coffee.  The goal is for you to leave with clarity and a process to implement to find or build investment in your business.  We'll check in on your branding, your mindset, your investor pack if you have one and create a process for you to start working on to find and nurture investors.  

  • Is this a course?

    At this time, no.  There is no course.  The Investor Ready Template gives you the actual Canva tool I use to present my investment opportunities in my business.  The 1:1 is a live discussion and brainstorming session.  

  • Can I just buy the pack?

    Yes!  If you don't want the 1:1 or don't feel quite ready yet, you can just purchase the Investor Ready Templates.  


    Just be aware, that if you book the 1:1 call, the templates are included in the price.  

  • Will I receive notes from my call?

    Of course it's better for you if you can take your own notes.  Notes will be sent over after the call to supliment your own.
