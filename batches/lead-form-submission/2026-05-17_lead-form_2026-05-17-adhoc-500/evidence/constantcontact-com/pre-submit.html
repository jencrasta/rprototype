Frequently asked questions

To create and send an email campaign with Constant Contact, start by selecting one of our hundreds of premade templates. Use our drag-and-drop editor and AI-powered branding tools to quickly assemble, draft, and customize content to match your business. Once your email is ready, preview what it will look like in the inbox on almost any device (with an upgrade) and hit send. You can also set up automated sending for specific events like welcome messages, birthday wishes, and resending to folks who don’t open your email the first time around. If you don't have an email list, gather contacts through social posting and advertising, sign-up forms, and landing pages — all offered through Constant Contact’s marketing platform.

Yes, we offer robust tools for attracting new customers, getting them onto your contact list, and segmenting your list for better personalization across all your marketing. You can segment your contact list based on behavior, engagement, and more. This helps you send more targeted, relevant content. 

Dynamic content blocks allow you to customize emails by showing or hiding content based on a recipient’s contact details like city, state, job title, or other custom fields, so you can make sure each person sees only what's relevant to them.

Constant Contact also integrates with hundreds of apps for enhanced segmentation options and a simpler, connected workspace that makes it easier to run your business.

Constant Contact offers a 30-day trial period where you can try our marketing tools for free. Beyond that, there are three plan options to choose from:

  • A Lite plan that’s $12 per month and includes drag-and-drop email editing, social posting, AI writing assistance, 300+ integrations, and a ready-to-go welcome series automation flow.

  • A Standard plan that’s $35 per month and includes everything in Lite, plus email and social post scheduling, three automation flows, email resending to non-openers, social advertising, and advanced reporting.

  • A Premium plan that’s $80 per month and includes everything in Standard, plus unlimited automation, unlimited custom segments, 500 text messages per month, lookalike ad targeting, and SEO recommendations.

Discounts are available to anyone who prepays up front. Pay for 6 months and get a 10% discount or pay for 12 months and get a 15% discount.

Nonprofits also get special prepay discounts: 20% off when they pay ahead for 6 months and 30% off when they pay ahead for 12 months.

Organizations with larger contact lists who need to send a higher volume of messages — such as companies with multiple teams, departments, or locations — can get custom pricing plans. Schedule a demo today to learn more.
