About Me

We can influence our reality with intention and awareness.

For the past five years, I’ve been immersed in studying tools and techniques to master my own life. The changes have been profound—not only for me, but for my family as well.  I’ve learned, without a doubt, that we are not at the mercy of fate. We can influence our reality with intention and awareness.

This belief inspired me to create Ba’Li (Balanced Life) and to reach you—my visitor—with my story, guiding you toward your own inner balance.

My mission is to contribute to raising people’s awareness on their potential by delivering this set of tools whenever it is needed. 

From stress to harmony, one breath at a time!
