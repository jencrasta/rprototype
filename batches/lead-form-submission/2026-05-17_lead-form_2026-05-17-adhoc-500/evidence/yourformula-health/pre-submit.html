MEET THE founders

dr. jonathan zaidan

suzana marku, Np-c

Founded by Dr. Jonathan Zaidan and Nurse Practitioner Suzana Marku, Your Formula was created to expand access to personalized, medically guided care through a modern telehealth model. With extensive clinical experience, they identified gaps in traditional healthcare delivery and aimed to provide a more efficient, patient-centered approach. The platform emphasizes comprehensive evaluation, evidence-based treatment, and ongoing support tailored to individual goals.

Your Formula was built to streamline the patient experience while maintaining clinical integrity and safety. Through structured consultations, appropriate lab testing, and individualized treatment plans, patients receive care that is both accessible and results-driven. The goal is to deliver consistent, high-quality outcomes by combining medical expertise with a simplified, technology-enabled care pathway.

common questions:

  • Your Formula is a virtual care platform connecting patients with licensed healthcare professionals. We specialize in personalized care for weight loss, hormone optimization, PCOS, hair restoration, sexual health, health optimization and skin rejuvenation — all from the comfort of your home.

    Through our streamlined process, you’ll connect with a licensed medical provider who will design a customized treatment plan delivered directly to your door.

  • Start by completing a quick online intake form to share your symptoms, goals, and medical history with your provider. A licensed healthcare professional will review your information and determine the most appropriate treatment plan for you.

    You’ll be prompted to schedule a virtual visit at a time that’s convenient for you. Once your provider confirms that treatment is right for you, if required, your prescription is shipped directly to your door in discreet, secure packaging—so you can start your plan with confidence and privacy.
