VIRTUAL PRODUCTION STUDIO LED WALL

Studio 54 now features the most cutting edge technology within the video production industry. Our spectacular LED Wall brings Hollywood quality Virtual Production to Madison Wisconsin. One of the largest of its kind available with production grade image quality, it's housed in our 30' x 30' x 50' production space allowing for flexibility. Our image based lighting system samples the backdrop images and video content to inform the RGBW lighting system dynamically. The studio is ready to go to process video and photo assets and soon will offer camera based motion tracking for the ultimate in virtual production led wall technology.
