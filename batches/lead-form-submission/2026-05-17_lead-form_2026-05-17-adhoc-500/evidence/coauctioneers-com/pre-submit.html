Welcome to the CAA

Are you an aspiring auctioneer or currently in the industry? If so, then you will want to join one of the best auctioneer associations in the country!

The Colorado Auctioneers Association exists to promote public auctions and the auction method of marketing through professional and ethical auctioneering practices in the state of Colorado. Membership is open to auctioneers, auction companies, and associates with an interest in this age-old method of marketing.

The Colorado Auctioneers Association (CAA) offers many member benefits including educational seminars, workshops & networking opportunities and we invite you to join us.

Colorado Auctioneers Foundation Scholarship
The Colorado Auctioneer’s Foundation (CAF) will award scholarship(s) at the annual, CAA Convention in January 2026. The object of these scholarships is to assist the recipients in getting a post-secondary education or, vocational / technical school. The scholarships cannot be used for CAI or other NAA designations.
