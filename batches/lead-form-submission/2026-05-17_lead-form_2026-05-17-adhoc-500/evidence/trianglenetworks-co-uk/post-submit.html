Services

Full-fibre connectivity

We provide a range of full-fibre services from CityFibre, Openreach and a number of other network providers, including FTTP, GPON and Ethernet services.

Copper &
part-fibre

Where full-fibre is not yet available, we can provide ADSL, FTTC, EoFTTC and G.fast services until your location is full-fibre ready.

Wireless connectivity

We offer WiFi, point-to-point and point-to-multi-point connections, as well as 4G SIM and mobile services.

Fixed line
telephony

We offer traditional fixed line services including analogue, ISDN2 and ISDN30 voice lines. We are also supporting our customers with the transition to IP voice.

Voice over IP
(VoIP)

Our flexible hosted telephony options and SIP services can be quickly deployed to support increasingly hybrid-working businesses.

Data centre
colocation

We can offer connectivity and data centre rack space in a number of locations across the Midlands and South of England.
