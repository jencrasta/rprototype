TRUSTED BY RETAILERS WORLDWIDE

Retail never stands still. Your operations shouldn’t trip over the basics.

Every store depends on essentials that customers never notice but rely on every day. Bags, boxes, labels, cleaning supplies, tools, safety items, fixtures, and POS materials. These products are GNFR — Goods Not For Resale — and they are critical for keeping your stores running.

The challenge? GNFR is usually spread across multiple suppliers, deliveries, and internal teams. That creates more admin, more complexity, and more risk of stockouts. It also leads to inconsistent budgets and a lack of visibility into what stores actually need.

GNFR may sit in the background, but it has a massive impact on the performance of your stores and the pressure on your teams.

Retail operations are complex enough. GNFR shouldn’t make them harder.

We come running in before your store runs out.

Worldpack brings all your retail essentials together so your teams don’t have to think about them. We source, consolidate, and deliver everything your stores rely on with the same urgency, accuracy, and care you expect from your own operation.

We simplify your GNFR by bringing structure, stock stability, and smarter logistics to the products that keep your stores open and your customers satisfied. This isn’t just supply. It’s a partnership built on decades of knowing how retail really works.
