Inspiring Young Athletes Since 2023


Super Athletes Ontario was founded in the spring of 2023 with a mission to help children stay active, build confidence, and discover their passion for sports. Since then, our summer camps have provided a dynamic and inclusive space where kids can explore different athletic activities, develop essential skills, and form lasting friendships.

Each summer, we welcome campers aged 5-13 from across Ontario and beyond. Our programs are designed to accommodate all skill levels, ensuring that every child regardless of background or experience has the opportunity to participate, grow, and thrive in a supportive environment

Through engaging sports programs, teamwork, and mentorship, Super Athletes Ontario is more than just a camp, it’s a community that empowers young athletes to reach their full potential
