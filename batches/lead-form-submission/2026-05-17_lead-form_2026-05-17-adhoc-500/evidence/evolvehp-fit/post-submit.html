    02 / The Methodology

    Others Guess.
    We Measure.

    Every program at Evolve starts with a baseline. We retest every 4–6 weeks. The numbers tell us what is working, what is not, and what comes next. That is why our athletes do not plateau, our young people live more confidently, our adults stop wondering if it is "working," and our seniors stand taller and walk steadier.

    /01

    Force-Plate Tested

    Hawkin Dynamics force plates, the same testing used by elite college athletic departments and pro teams. Jump performance, asymmetry, power output. Numbers, not vibes.

    /02

    Speed Verified

    Universal Speed Rating (USR) certified lab. Third-party verified speed scores recruiters trust, and the only way to know if your training is actually making you faster.

    /03

    Heart-Rate Coached

    Polar heart rate on every athlete, every session. The right intensity, every time. No guessing, no junk volume, no overtraining.

    /04

    Tracked & Retested

    Lumin Sports platform stores your full history. Every 4–6 weeks we retest. The graph either goes up or we change the plan. That is the entire deal.

    Why It Works

    Reduced Injury Risk.More Commitment.Better Results.

    Strong, supported athletes train more. Athletes who train more get better, faster. The math is not complicated, but the environment that makes it possible is rare.

    At Evolve, we screen for asymmetries before they become injuries. We coach in small groups so no rep is uncoached. And we build a culture where kids ask questions, adults are not embarrassed by their starting point, and seniors get treated like the athletes they still are. The result: people stay. People show up. People get better.

    /01

    Screen, don't guess

    Data flags risks weeks before symptoms appear, so we can reinforce the weak side before it becomes an injury.

    /02

    Coach, don't watch

    Small groups of 10–12 max, so every rep gets corrected, every cue is specific, and every effort is seen.

    /03

    Build, don't break

    Progressive overload tied to retests. We add load only when the numbers say you are ready for it.

    /04

    Belong, don't compete

    A floor where 14-year-olds and 64-year-olds both thrive. Heads down, ego at the door, working at their own pace.
