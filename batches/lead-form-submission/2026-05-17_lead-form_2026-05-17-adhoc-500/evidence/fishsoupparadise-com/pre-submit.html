Our Story

Mr. Wu Sin Min (Jerry), the brainchild of Fish Soup Paradise. His eldest daughter (age 9) in love of Fish Soup made by him inspired him to venture into his first F&B Business offering Fish Soup varieties at online store and 2 outlets in the Central Business District in Singapore since year 2020. This Fish Soup is a family recipe from Mr. Wu great grandpa and had been all time favourite to neighboring folks that tasted Mr. Wu family Fish Soup. Today Fish Soup Paradise Own 2 outlets and have its Food Factory at 20 Mactaggart Road.
