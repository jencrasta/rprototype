BESPOKE ORDERS 

At Hats World, we specialise in bringing your unique brand vision to life through fully bespoke headwear and accessories. Unlike standard suppliers who simply embroider on top of existing "blank" stock, we build your products from the ground up—fabric, fit, stitching, and hardware—to your exact specifications.

How We Work As direct importers managing a global supply chain, we operate differently than traditional local print shops. We bridge the gap between your brand and the factory floor.

Understanding Our Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs) Because we manufacture custom goods overseas to ensure premium quality and competitive pricing, our process requires higher Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs) than domestic suppliers.

  • The Trade-off: While this means we cannot accommodate small, on-demand runs (e.g., 10–50 units), it allows us to offer significantly lower unit costs and virtually limitless customization options.

  • The Benefit: You get a retail-quality product that is 100% unique to your brand, produced at a volume that maximizes your profit margins
