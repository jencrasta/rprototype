Services

We take great pride in our great customer service, trained event staff, brand partnerships, and drop-ship fulfillment capabilities.

On-Site Fitting Events

We offer on-site fitting events with brand name watches, jewelry, sunglasses, handbags, wallets, fragrances and much more. We bring a wide selection of styles at desired price points.

Automated Product Feeds

We can deliver daily product feeds via email, or to an FTP site upon request. The feeds include high res images, pricing, and inventory levels. We are also EDI capable.

Drop Ship Fulfillment Services

We offer affordable, fast and reliable drop ship fulfillment for your drop ship programs. We ship daily with UPS, FedEx, and USPS.

Private Label Watches

Castle Merchandising is a leading designer, manufacturer, and distributor for high quality watches. Our commitment to quality in our design and manufacturing has given us a loyal clientele. We would be happy to help you with your next project. Please contact us for a quote, samples, and free virtual proofs.

In-house fulfillment

Our highly trained warehouse team provides our client’s the highest accuracy rates possible in the industry. We have scheduled pickups daily with FedEx, USPS, and UPS. We are prepared to handle shipping out thousands of packages per day, but very happy to assist just taking care of one drop shipment too. No minimum order required! We look forward to assisting with your drop shipment, and/or bulk ordering shipping needs.

EDI capable

EDI stands for Electronic Data Interchange and loosely defined, EDI is the structured transmission of electronic data such as purchase orders, invoices and shipping notices between organizations. We are fully EDI capable, and proud to offer this technology to our clients.
