🏠🏠Toronto Regional Real Estate Board - March 2026 🏠🏠 “It’s encouraging to see an uptick in March home sales compared to last month and last year. This suggests that an increasing number of GTA households are looking to take advantage of improved affordability as we move into the spring market. Positive news on trade and geopolitical issues would help improve consumer confidence and home sales in the months ahead,” said TRREB President Daniel Steinfeld. “Buyers continued to benefit from substantial negotiating power on price across major market segments in the last month.
