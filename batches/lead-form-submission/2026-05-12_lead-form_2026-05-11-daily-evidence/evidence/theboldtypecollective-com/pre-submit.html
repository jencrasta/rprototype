Let’s Talk — Human to Human.

Hey — thanks for your interest in working with The Bold Type Collective.

Clarity, alignment, and messaging that actually lands.

We use this form to make sure we’re the right fit for what you need — and to make the most of your time, energy, and investment.

How It Works:

1. Apply to Work With Me

Start by filling out this short form to share where you’re at and what kind of support you’re seeking. It helps me get a sense of your brand, your message, and your goals — no fluff, just truth.

2. Book a Free Clarity Call

Once your form is submitted, you’ll receive a link to book a free 30-minute consult. We’ll chat through your vision, any gaps or questions, and what aligned support could look like.

3. Lock It In

If we’re a fit, I’ll send over a custom proposal with options tailored to your brand. You’ll choose your path, sign your agreement, and secure your start date. Let’s build something that feels as good as it looks.

Who This Is For / Not For

This is for you if:

  • You’re clear on your vision, but struggling to articulate it

  • Your message feels scattered across platforms or teams

  • You want strategic clarity, not surface-level content

  • You value thoughtful collaboration over quick fixes

.

This may not be for you if:

  • You’re looking for one-off execution without strategy

  • You want instant results without internal alignment

  • You’re not ready to invest time or intention into your messaging
