WHY BECOME A MEMBER OF THE SMTA?

Join over 1,300 other Scottish Motor Industry businesses today – and get exclusive access to a host of benefits.

For over 120 years we have been at the forefront of the Scottish Motor Industry. We are member-owned, member led and member driven and have been ever since our inception way back in 1903.

Get access to five exclusive industry services

We offer members a host of exclusive industry benefits through our five key services

EXCLUSIVE ACCESS TO A SCOTTISH-BASED USED CAR WARRANTY COMPANY

EXCLUSIVE ACCESS TO GENUINE TRADE VOLUME DEALS AS PART OF A UNIQUE BUYING GROUP

OUR MEMBERSHIP TAKE A LOCAL, RESPONSIBLE APPROACH TO THE ON-GOING SKILLS REQUIREMENTS OF THEIR BUSINESS

EXCLUSIVE ACCESS TO INDUSTRY LEADING MOT QMS AND REPRESENTATION

BE PART OF THE FUTURE FOR TRANSPORT ACROSS SCOTLAND AND PROTECT YOUR BUSINESS.

Your membership has a host of benefits to aid your business

The SMTA MOT QMS Programme is recognised across the motor industry as providing a level of Quality Control that exceeds the minimum standards set by DVSA.

One of our most valuable services is MOT Support and Representation. We provide direct support to members who experience a challenge from DVSA. Our team is experienced at working with DVSA and our in-depth knowledge helps to ensure the most positive outcomes possible are achieved.

Our Conciliation Service, available and free to members, means that when and if things go wrong you know you have the support you need to resolve disputes as positively as possible.

SMTA (Trading Partners) Ltd, our Buying Group, is available only to members. It provides reduced pricing on a number of motor trade products and services. SMTA membership could provide significant savings for your business.

The exclusive to members Motor Trade Insurance offer brings an experienced team, familiar with motor trade requirements, which can assess your requirements and deliver reduced charges and improved benefits.

The SMTA Apprentice Training Services provide structured courses and ensures that your business gains people with the expertise needed for successful operations. We can manage all of your apprentice training needs.

Support is also available through our HR/Employment and Health & Safety Advice Services which can reduce the risks and costs associated with providing a safe and compliant working environment.

Our Waste Management provider provides substantial discounts exclusively to our members. There are options to pick standard or specifically tailored solutions to deal with your waste and deliver income to your business.

    SMTA CODE OF CONDUCT*
    The SMTA Code of Conduct ensuring that clients interacting with us will be
    protected by this Code, this is in addition to THEIR statutory rights.
    • We subscribe to the following principles:
    • To provide competent trained personnel to ensure an efficient, consistent high
    quality customer experience at all times.
    • To be open, fair and transparent in all our business dealings.
    • To, upon request, provide written estimates and details for all services to be
    performed or products provided, these will show Value Added Tax where relevant.
    • To obtain prior consent before undertaking additional works.
    • To attend to our customers’ needs in a professional manner.
    • To operate our business in accordance with the highest standards of ethical and
    moral conduct.
    • To advertise our products in a positive,factual and informative manner.
    • To undertake to resolve customer complaints in a prompt and courteous manner.
    • To introduce, if appropriate, the SMTA’s Conciliation Service and when required
    provide access to the Alternative Dispute Resolution service used by the
    Association.
    We recognise and accept our responsibility to maintain high standards of business
    ethics and integrity as members of the Scottish Motor Trade Association
