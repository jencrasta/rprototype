Film & Photography

Cambridge has provided the setting to some great films over the years and it’s an incredibly photogenic city. In most Colleges and venues, you’re welcome to undertake photography, filming and illustration for private, non-commercial use.

Permission to photograph or film professionally, or for commercial purposes, must be sought in advance. To apply for permits, please contact the venue directly, with as much notice as possible. If you would like to film and take photographs of a College, please apply as soon as possible as some requests need will need to go to the College Council, which can take some time.

The below venues provide extra information when looking to film on site:

Jesus College:

Apply to Film, Photograph and Audio Record

Newnham College:

Filming, Photography and Audio Recording Policy

St Catharine's College:

Filming, Photography and Audio Recording Policy

 

Find out more about Cambridge in popular culture, including films shot on location in the city on Wikipedia.

About Cambridge

Meet Cambridge is the conference and events bureau for Cambridge and the official venue-finding service for space at The University of Cambridge and the Cambridge Colleges. Its extensive portfolio also includes hotels and unique properties in and around the city, providing creative and imaginative settings for all types of events.
