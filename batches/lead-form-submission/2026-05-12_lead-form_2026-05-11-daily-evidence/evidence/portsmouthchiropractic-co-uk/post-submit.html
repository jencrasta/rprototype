Experienced Chiropractor

With over 17 years in practice, Katherine, an experienced chiropractor has successfully treated a whole range of conditions. We don’t simply treat low back and neck pain, we also carefully assess and help to treat many other conditions affecting shoulders, knees, hips and much more…so relax, you are in good hands.

SUPPORT & HOME ADVICE

It is not enough to simply address your condition and treat it in clinic.  We will always go the extra mile to ensure you fully understand your condition, how it happened and most importantly how to prevent it happening in the future. We will never send you away with a standard sheet of exercises because you are are unique, and so is your condition.

WHY CHOOSE US?

WE BELIEVE IN THE VERY BEST.

Our approach is both evidence-based and ethical, ensuring that your progress is expedited through a combination of treatment, rehabilitation, lifestyle advice and most importantly, how you can help yourself. After all, your progress and recovery is a mutual partnership between your practitioner and yourself to get well and stay well.

We work closely with other health professionals, and are very proud of our local reputation, receiving referrals from many local GP’s and orthopaedic consultants alike, assured of a professional service as they know their patients are in excellent hands.

Best of all, we don’t accept patients for care unless we truly believe this is the right place for them. It is only after we have completed a thorough consultation, examination and explained the findings to you (so that you understand your complaint fully) that we can help you make some decisions about your health and how best get optimum results in the quickest possible time frame.

GENEROUS TREATMENT TIMES AS STANDARD
