Urgent care, with women in mind.

Daisy Women’s Center & OB Urgent Care was created to meet a very real need in Atlanta—fast, specialized care for women that doesn’t require waiting days for an appointment or hours in an emergency room. Our center is designed specifically for women experiencing urgent gynecologic, reproductive, and early pregnancy concerns. From pelvic pain and abnormal bleeding to urinary tract infections and vaginal infections, our team provides prompt evaluation, on-site testing, and same-day treatment in a private, supportive environment. By focusing exclusively on women’s health, we deliver a level of expertise and comfort that traditional urgent care centers often cannot provide.

Daisy Women’s Center also addresses commonly overlooked areas of care, including postpartum concerns and women’s mental health. New mothers can receive evaluation and treatment for issues such as breast pain, mastitis, elevated blood pressure, and mood changes without waiting weeks for a follow-up appointment. Additionally, women experiencing anxiety, stress, or emotional overwhelm can access timely support and guidance. By integrating physical and mental health services, we provide a more comprehensive model of urgent care for women at every stage of life.
